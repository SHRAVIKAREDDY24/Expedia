January - Jaipur

Description

January is an excellent time to visit Jaipur because of the Kite Festival and also the Jaipur Literature Festival that's held around this time. Lying in sync with the Jaipur Literature fest, January is the perfect time to take a trip to the royal land. Get yourself clicked amidst the forts and palaces, gorge on some authentic Rajasthani meals and shop like a princess. Evenings are generally cold in this season. This can be an ideal time for tourists to go for shopping and other tourist activities even in the afternoon. One of the major festival- Jaipur Literature Fest occurs during January and attracts people from different parts of the world. However Jaipur is one of the beautiful cities in India you must visit and offers lot of tourist attractions: Amer Fort, Hawa Mahal, City Palace, Jantar Mantar etc.

February - Nashik

Description

If the caves and temples do not inspire you to leave for Nashik then the Sula fest will definitely do. It will offer you get a chance to groove to some good music, taste a variety of wines and know the latest fashion trends.Known for its religious and historical importance, Nashik is best visited in February when the city experiences the winter season. In general, the town has extreme weather conditions. So the summers are really hot, and winters are chilly. The summer temperature can touch or go beyond 39°C whereas a drastic drop in the mercury level is seen during the peak winters; going as low as 5°C. Thus, winters are best if one wishes to explore the cityscape uninterruptedly. Between July and September, the land of the Indian Wine receives the right amount of rainfall that transforms the city as the greenery flourishes, making it a treat to visit, especially for monsoon lovers and offbeat travellers.

March - Mathura-Vrindavan

Description

‘Holi’ – the festival of colors is one of the major Festivals celebrated in India. Well known for its beautiful traditions, Holi is one of the most vivid celebrations of India, which is celebrated with full energy, zeal, excitement, and enthusiasm everywhere throughout the nation. Traditional Holi celebrations are the biggest ones in Mathura, and Vrindavan, the birth city of Lord Krishna. Although, it is celebrated all over India yet the festival is celebrated with huge pomp & show at the places related to tales of Lord Krishna & Radha.The holy city of Mathura, the origin place of Lord Krishna, remains at its best during the celebration of Holi. On the day of the festival, the best place to visit in Mathura is the Dwarkadheesh Temple.

April - Shillong

Description

Get a countryside feel as you cycle through the lanes of Shillong. The scenic beauty with all the flowers blooming at their best in April make it an ideal place to holiday away from the hotness of North India. Shillong is the wondrous capital city of Meghalaya, whose name literally means “the abode of the clouds”. So, you can only imagine the nature, beauty and scenery that it proudly possesses. It’s a must-visit destination for anyone wanting to explore the North-East, acting as a picturesque gateway to the region and all it has to offer. Sitting at a height of 1496 metres above sea level, Shillong experiences a different climate to much of tropical India — that’s why it is often called the “Scotland of the East”. The rains always put on a show and tend to overstay their welcome.

May - Ladakh

Description

May is the peak tourist season in Ladakh and traveling during this month will offer you a memorable experience of your life. The weather is warm with a cool breeze, which makes May one of the best months to visit Ladakh. And if you are traveling to this land of adventures, we have a comprehensive guide to help you plan a trip to Ladakh in May. You will see an influx of tourists from around the globe in Ladakh who visit here and gain the most cherishable memories of their life. From avid bike rides to families, friend groups to solo travelers, and even families and honeymooners prefer to visit Ladakh for a summer vacation. The things to keep in mind while traveling to Ladakh in May, clothes to pack, routes that are open, weather conditions, local transportation, and accommodation.

June - Dharamshala

Description

Dharamshala is a beautiful city located in Kangra district. Popular with tourists world over, it has the unique Dhauladhar ranges as a picturesque backdrop, for fun and adventure activities. It also has one of the best cricket stadiums in the country, the Dharamshala Cricket Stadium, where you should not miss watching a match if there happens to be one when you are around. The cool weather backed with a very serene and natural habitat will make it an awesome experience. What will also attract your attention are the places to visit in Dharamshala is June like ancient temples, monasteries, and more. You can also stop by small shops selling all sorts of Tibetan artifacts since Dharamshala is also the host city of the Tibetan government in exile and home to the Tibetan Spiritual leader the Dalai Lama.

July - Dalhousie

Description

Dalhousie, with its ideal climatic conditions, makes for one of the very few places which may be visited all year round. However, the best time to visit Dalhousie is from the month of june-july . The snow begins to melt in the months of March and April and its brilliance can be seen when the rays of sun hit the snow-capped mountains and pastures. The weather remains rather pleasant during this period and you can continue to experience the chills too. The temperature begins to rise in the month of July. The summer months experience nice weather throughout, thus making it a perfect destination for nature buffs. Monsoons are also quite pleasant here as the moderate rainfall does not cause any inconvenience to your plans. Winters in Dalhousie are perfect for outdoor activities.

August - Kanyakumari

Description

The southernmost tip of India, Kanyakumari in Tamil Nadu is a popular tourist destination offering fantastic sunrise sunset views. Thiruvalluvar Statue and Vivekanand Rock Memorial are two important places to visit in Kanyakumari. The beautiful city of Kanyakumari is located at the southernmost tip of India. Besides the popularity of this geographical location, the place is a renowned centre for art and culture, and is also a busy pilgrimage destination. With so many reasons to add Kanyakumari to the bucket list, it’s a great thing that the place experiences a moderate climate all throughout the year. Early mornings and evenings are cooler. Kanyakumari gets a heavy monsoon and it makes the town even more beautiful. Kanyakumari is undoubtedly a beautiful destination for a vacation.

September - Lonavala

Description

The best thing about Lonavala and Khandala is the pleasant weather all year round. The best time to visit is June to September, during monsoon season, when surroundings come alive in beautiful lush greenery, and the lakes get filled up to the brim with the rain. From October to mid-March, you would need light woollens, especially for the evenings. It’s a great time to go sightseeing and indulge in outdoor activities such as trekking and hiking. The winter months are also quite popular with honeymooners.A destination for relaxing vacation, Lonavala & Khandala are excellent for hiking, trekking and enjoying peaceful time by the cascading waterfalls. There are numerous historical forts, ancient caves, famous dams and beautiful lakes which makes it a popular tourist spot.

October - Andamans

Description

The temperature in Andaman is suitable for a trip year-round. There is no summer and winter in Andaman, the temperature remains the same throughout the year. It is just that the humidity starts to decrease in Andaman in October. In fact, October marks the start of the peak tourist season. The monsoon showers are gone by September-end and the intermittent rains as well as moisture-laden breeze keep the weather pleasant. A pleasant weather with mild sun but cool winds are the perfect time to explore the best of Andaman. There are a lot of luring temptations that will attract you to this mesmerizing island of Andaman in October, from underwater walking, bird watching, scuba diving, to glass boat rides, trekking, star gazing, and cruise rides.

November - Alleppey

Description

The best time to visit Alleppey is between October and February when the temperature is moderate (around 33°C), making it a perfect time to explore the beautiful town. Alleppey experiences monsoons between June and September. It is not advisable for tourists, but the monsoon lovers and the offbeat travellers can consider visiting in June. November to February is also the best time to visit Alleppey if you're on your honeymoon or if you want to enjoy the famous houseboats of Alleppey or spend some time lazying on the beach. Summers are also not recommended for the extreme heat that hits the region as the temperature can go as high as 40°C shutting all the outdoor activities. The town receives light rainfall occasionally that takes the mercury level down allowing tourists to explore the attractions.

December - GOA

Description

December is peak season in Goa, so there are far more people on the beaches. The top beaches in North Goa – Baga, Candolim, Calangute and Anjuna can get quite crowded, especially during the weekends, the Christmas and New Year time. The South is relatively quiet, but there are more tourists here too. Cooler than November and more pleasant. It rains for just 1 day this month in an average year. Beautiful sunny weather as Goa receives 10 hours of sunlight every day. The average temperature is 32°C, about the same as November. A pleasant wind blows in from the sea. Best time of the year at Goa. A hot party month in Goa(Sunburn,Supersonic Festival,Mando Festival, etc). There are parties everywhere, beach shacks, and even right on the beaches. The south is relatively quieter, but after sundown, the south too comes alive with parties.
